[size=24][b][color=#ffd700]Gear Sets and Transition Optimization[/color][/b][/size]
[div align="center"][url=#section-top][color=#00BFFF]Back to Top[/color][/url][/div]

[div style="background-color:#1a1a1a; padding:20px; border-radius:15px; margin:15px 0;"]

[size=18][b][color=#ffa500]➤ Introduction[/color][/b][/size]

[color=#ffffff]
Gear optimization is essential for any Paladin aiming to maximize performance in FFXI. Whether you use the vanilla game’s EquipSets system or advanced tools like GearSwap, your goal is the same: seamless gear transitions with minimal HP fluctuations to maintain stability for healers and ensure optimal performance in all situations.

This guide is divided into two parts:
- [b][color=#FFD700]Vanilla EquipSets Users:[/color][/b] For those using the in-game EquipSets feature without any external tools.
- [b][color=#FFD700]GearSwap Users:[/color][/b] For advanced users leveraging GearSwap scripts for precise control and prioritization during gear swaps.
[/color]

---

[size=18][b][color=#ffa500]➤ Vanilla EquipSets Users[/color][/b][/size]

[color=#ffffff]
For players relying on the vanilla game’s EquipSets system, gear changes occur instantly, so the order of swaps doesn’t matter. Instead, your focus should be on balancing your gear sets to maintain consistent HP across transitions.

[b][color=#ffd700]Key Tips for Vanilla Players:[/color][/b]
- [b]Minimize HP fluctuations:[/b] Ensure the difference between your highest and lowest HP sets does not exceed **400 HP**.
- [b]Balance your sets:[/b] Use gear with similar HP values across Idle, Fast Cast, and Full Enmity sets.
- [b]Simplify your setup:[/b] Focus on creating sets that work well in a variety of scenarios without frequent manual adjustments.

By following these guidelines, you can ensure stable performance without relying on external tools or scripts.
[/color]

---

[size=18][b][color=#ffa500]➤ GearSwap Users[/color][/b][/size]

[color=#ffffff]
For advanced users using GearSwap, prioritization during gear swaps becomes critical. By defining priorities for each gear slot, you can control the order in which items are swapped, ensuring minimal HP fluctuations and stable transitions.

[b][color=#ffd700]Why Prioritization Matters:[/color][/b]
- [b]Prevents sudden HP drops:[/b] This ensures healers are not caught off guard during transitions.
- [b]Optimizes stability:[/b] Reduces the risk of dying during gear changes in critical moments.
- [b]Enables precision:[/b] You can fine-tune your gear swaps to maximize efficiency.

Below, we provide detailed examples of how to define your sets in GearSwap, including prioritization for each slot.
[/color]
